About Me

I'm Adam McCarthy, a software engineer at Blend, where I work on the Connectivity team. I'm also an alumnus of the Software Engineering program at Rochester Institute of Technology. This is my personal webpage. Here you'll find details about my experience and various projects I've worked on. If you'd like to contact me, you can use the links above to do so.

Most of my experience is with web and microservice development, although I have worked with standalone desktop applications client-server applications as well. I have the most experience in heavily object-oriented languages such as Java, although I'm comfortable working in a wide variety of langauges including compiled languages like Golang and C, and scripting languages, including JavaScript, Python, and Groovy.
