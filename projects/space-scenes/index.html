Space Scenes

An window into space, right on your home screen.

Space Scenes is an Android widget which uses NASA's Astronomy Photo of the Day API to show a different astronomy-related photo on your home screen each day. Space Scenes was developed with Val Booth for the NASA Space Apps Challenge.

While the primary goal of the application is to view astronomy images from your home screen, you can click on the widget to view information about the image, such as its name, a short summary or story, and the photographer/artist, as well as an enlarged version of the photo.

The source code for Space Scenes is available on GitHub.
